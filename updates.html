Welcome to 21 Days!

21 Days is an game that tells the story of a teenager who experiences a severe brain injury whist taking very high doses of LSD.

His injured brain amplifies the effects of the LSD, blending reality with an infinite tripping dream.

This new world is dangerous, bringing his deepest horrors to life from the deepest, darkest corners of this new tripping universe.

Will you survive? Will you live to see the world normally again?
